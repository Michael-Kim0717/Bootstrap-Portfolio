ABOUT ME


Hello, thank you for visiting my page! :)

My name is Michael Kim. I graduated from Rutgers University in 2017 with a Bachelor's in Computer Science and self-studied mobile development with the intentions of building productivity applications. I am currently on track to graduate from the Rutgers Coding Bootcamp in the fall of 2018 to become a full-stack developer. As I continue to learn and progress, I am hoping to become an all-around developer.

Aside from programming, I am also a 3rd degree black belt, aspiring baker/chef, and Sunday school teacher. When I was young, I always had a love for cooking (even though I knew nothing of it), drawing, and astronomy. I sought to use make my imaginations come to life. Even though my level of creativity has gone down, I am determined to allow myself to explore my options once again through my hobbies.

Some of my recent aspirations for the future have been to travel around the world, become a better baker/chef, learn some acrobatics, and become an engineer that is more versatile, but knowledgeable in the languages I already know.
