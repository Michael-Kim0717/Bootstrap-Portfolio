ABOUT ME


Hello, thank you for visiting my page! :)

My name is Michael Kim. I graduated from Rutgers University in 2017 with a Bachelor's in Computer Science. While in school, I have been studying mobile development with the intentions of becoming a software developer.

Aside from programming, I am also a 3rd degree black belt, aspiring baker, and Sunday school teacher. When I was young, I always had a love for cooking (even though I knew nothing of it) and astronomy. As of now, my recent passion has just been seeing things come together. Whether it has been projects, creation of a dish, event planning coming to life, or even a simple 1000-piece puzzle set, I highly enjoyed the process. Just being able to see the fruits of labor come to life has been my most recent joy center.

Some of my future goals are traveling the world, becoming a good chef, and learning some acrobatics.
